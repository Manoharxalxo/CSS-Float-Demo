India had 112 unicorns at the end of the last fiscal year with a collective valuation of over $350 billion, according to Invest India data, reports businessline. Only two startups joined the unicorn club in 2023, compared to 22 in 2022. Indians have also founded the highest number of offshore unicorns, reports The Economic Times, citing Hurun Research Institute's Global Unicorn Index 2024.

"The audio series phenomenon is currently ablaze," writes Vineet Singh, Vice President, Brand and Communications at Pocket FM in a post on LinkedIn. He adds that in a world where videos usually steal the spotlight, hitting the big ₹100 crore mark — which the audio platform did with two of its audio series — is a significant benchmark that symbolises massive success for films in India.
